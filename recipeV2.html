Pannukakku

Pannukakku is a classic Finnish baked pancake rooted in rural farmhouse cooking of the 1800s, shaped by Swedish influence but distinct in texture and method.

Link to history

This recipe is similar to pancakes but is different in its texture and shape, as it is more fluffy and shaped more rectangular and thick.

Ingredients

Link to recipe
  • Milk – 400ml
  • 2 eggs
  • Flour – 200ml
  • Sugar – 1 tbsp
  • Salt – 1/2 tsp
  • Vanilla sugar – 1 tsp optional
  • Butter 2–3 tbsp + extra for the pan

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 225°C (435°F).
  2. Mix the batter:
    • Whisk eggs and milk together.
    • Add flour, sugar, salt, and vanilla sugar.
    • Mix until smooth (small lumps are fine).
    • Stir in melted butter.
  3. Butter a baking tray or oven dish generously.
  4. Pour batter into tray.
  5. Bake for 20–30 minutes.
  6. Cool for a few minutes before serving.
